Frequently Asked Questions

Is Meru School Really that good ?

Meru School is among the best National schools in Kenya that you can trust with your child. We offer a wide variety of extra carricular activities with lots of departments for students.

Any Notable Alumni for Meru School?

Meru school has been producing the best of the best students nationally. our efforts has seen us boast of a rich selection of Alumni both nationally and internationally.

How digitalised is the school administration?

Most of our administration processes have been digitalised to help in effective running of the school. You can do an online admission via our online admission form.

Is the school specific to any religion?

No. The School is not limited to any religion as per the student concern. We accept and nature all available religions in Kenya.
